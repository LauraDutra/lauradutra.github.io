Things That I Do

I treat every project as a collaboration. I’ve helped authors, speakers, educators, and other clients to launch or refine their businesses and make it work for their specific needs. I’m only one person, but I help clients in several very specific areas.

Brand Development & Logo Design

Starting from scratch or give a new look to a product or service. I work with you to create a visual representation of your brand and product personality. You even get a sweet package with all the files and resources you need to make your brand anywhere.

Marketing & Social Media Graphics

Campaigns of all shapes and sizes need to look their best! From website landing pages, ebooks, and social media images - you're covered. I even offer to work on retainer with you (or just show you the ropes) on how to keep your social media current and relevant.

Wordpress Website Design & Development

Strategy, design, & (primarily) WordPress website development. Full websites, from start to finish. I take care of the nerdy pieces like mobile-responsiveness, structure and flow, plus some training bits before sending you on your way.
